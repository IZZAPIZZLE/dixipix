# BRAINSTORM — Context

## What this workspace is for
Raw thinking. Strategy. Ideas. Nothing filtered, nothing lost.
Text only. No images, no code.

## What lives here
- `IDEAS.md` — running list of ideas, changes, directions
- `STRATEGY.md` — positioning, pricing, offers
- Any rough notes or thinking-out-loud docs

## Process
Open IDEAS.md and dump. Don't filter.
Review when making decisions about the business or site direction.

## What does NOT live here
No client records. No code. No media.
